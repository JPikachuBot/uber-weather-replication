# Uber Weather Margin Analysis — Replication & Benchmark

This repository replicates and extends Jackson Turnbull's original analysis of Uber pricing behavior during weather events at LaGuardia Airport (LGA), using NYC TLC rideshare trip data (Jan–Sep 2025) merged with NOAA hourly weather observations. The central business question is: how much margin is Uber leaving on the table during weather events at LGA, and what pricing change would recover it? The replication adds a dollar-figure opportunity estimate and an experiment design — outputs missing from the original work — to produce a complete, defensible business case for Uber's Pricing or Marketplace team.

See `REPLICATION.md` for phase-by-phase execution instructions and `deliverable/business_case.md` for the final output.
